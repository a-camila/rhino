Rhinoceros

There are five species of rhinoceros: white, black, Indian, Javan, and Sumatran.

  • Scientific Name: Rhinocerotidae
  • Average Length: 8-13 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 35-50 years
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical grasslands, savannas, shrublands, and forests
